Email Signature Templates

Instructions

Follow the instructions below carefully to implement the "Taking you places!" email signature for Outlook.

Starting in Outlook:
  1. In the main Outlook menu bar, navigate to File > Options
  2. Under Outlook Options, under the Mail tab, click on "Signatures..."
  3. Click "New" to start a new signature, give it a name and click "OK"
  4. Using the links below, select the signaure for your Mode (this will open in a new tab)
  5. Important: On the signature page Select All by pressing Ctrl + A
  6. Copy the selection by pressing Ctrl + C
  7. Return to your newly created signature in Outlook, and press Ctrl + V to paste the template in the editor window
  8. Modify the placeholder text with your personal contact information
  9. Click Save when finished

Be sure to select your new signature under "Choose default signature" in the E-mail Signatures tab.

Feel free to adjust the font size once you have created your signature.
